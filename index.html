Why These Sizes?

Formulas Used
1. Appliance Energy Energy per appliance = Power (W) × Hours of use per day
2. Total Daily Energy Total daily energy = Sum of all appliance energies in Wh/day
3. Panel Size Panel power needed = Total daily energy ÷ Peak sun hours ÷ 0.85
4. Battery Capacity Battery energy = Total daily energy × 1 day autonomy
5. Inverter Size Inverter power = Total running load × 1.2, then convert to kVA
6. Estimated Current Current = Total power ÷ System voltage. Here system voltage is 24V
